WE LOVE PLAYING FASTPITCH SOFTBALL!

Welcome to our team website! We are a group of high school girls from Topeka and the surrounding area who love playing softball!

There are so many benefits to playing softball! It is a fantastic workout! It's a game that improves self-esteem. It's good for your mental health. Softball is great for muscle developement. It lays the groundwork for successful relationships and it gives girls a chance to develop their leadership skills!

We have a wonderful support group of coaches, parents, grandparents and friends that enjoy the sport too! We practice year round, indoors and outdoors and even travel to other cities to compete with other teams! We have so much fun! Feel free to have a look around our site to see what we are all about!
